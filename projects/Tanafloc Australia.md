Tanafloc Australia is a specialist industrial waste-water treatment product with a more scientific focus than a typical retail website. I worked across design and development, using Figma as a primary design tool before coordinating parts of the build with an offshore developer. I also handled hosting and DNS migration, which made this one of my first projects covering the full delivery path from design through to launch.

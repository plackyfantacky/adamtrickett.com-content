Armour Homes is a home builder and granny flat builder that originally had three separate Webflow websites. I consolidated the sites into a single Webflow build, created a new design from existing visual elements, and migrated the content into Webflow CMS. The project was a useful exercise in design consolidation, content migration, and working through the limits of Webflow on a more complex site.

Guarda Systems is a product range of concrete and wall cutting machinery, with a strong focus on reducing hazardous silica dust. I developed the site in WordPress and Elementor, using WooCommerce as the structure for managing product information rather than online sales. The initial design direction had been started offshore, and I continued the design across additional pages while completing the build.
